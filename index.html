Introducing the AI-powered assessment tool

The Sorting Hat is more than a personality quiz.
It's a way of identifying future potential.

Fast

Taking The Sorting Hat Test only takes about 15 minutes and results are processed immediately. There is a combination of multiple choice and text-based questions.

Simple

Taking the test is easy: simply click the button to take the test immediately. No sign-up fee, no subscription and no account required!

Flexible

Results from the test can be stored on-premises or on the cloud. Custom question banks can also be prepared upon request, though this may affect The Sorting Hat's overall accuracy.

Secure

Access to the results can be tailored to meet your security needs. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit, ensuring that any sensitive details will be protected.
