Where to next for The Sorting Hat?

We foresee that The Sorting Hat could also be a useful tool for universities and for current law students. Anecdotal evidence has shown that students are often overwhelmed with choices while at university, and many do not know of the full extent of the diversity of discplines in law. Therefore, The Sorting Hat may also prove useful to help law students to discover potential areas of law that may suit them, which can inform elective choices and identify potential pathways.
